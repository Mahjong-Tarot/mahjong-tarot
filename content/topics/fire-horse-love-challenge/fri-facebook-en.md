# Friday Facebook EN — Fire Horse Love Challenge

**Weekend challenge:** Look at your relationship from a new angle.

Not "do I love them?" — you know that. Not "are we happy?" — surface question.

Ask this instead: do we balance each other?

Where one of you is strong — emotionally, practically, energetically — is the other at least present? Not equal. Not identical. But present. Bringing something real to that dimension.

That's what lasting looks like in Chinese astrology. Not completion — "they have what I lack." Balance — "we are each enough to choose each other from strength instead of need."

This week we talked about opposing signs and what the Fire Horse year does to relationships built on contrast. And we talked about the second angle of the Mahjong Mirror — who you are, who you need to become, what you truly want — and what it reveals about love that chemistry never tells you.

The challenge is to take five minutes this weekend, quietly, and answer the balance question honestly. For your relationship or for the one you're looking for.

Where are you strong? Where is your partner strong? Where do the charts overlap, and where are you genuinely leaning on each other to fill gaps neither of you has?

You don't have to share the answer with anyone. But write it down. Because in a Fire Horse year, the things you don't look at honestly have a way of making themselves impossible to ignore.

Happy weekend. Go tend the garden.
