There's no shortcut to understanding a relationship. It's more than "do we get along." It's more than "do we have chemistry." It's more than "are our signs compatible."

The Mahjong Mirror looks at the full picture. Five elements. Yin-yang balance. What's driving the relationship underneath the surface. Who each person actually is versus who they're showing up as.

That last one (who you actually are versus who you're showing up as) is the most important. Because if you're performing a version of yourself that this relationship drew out of you, eventually the performance gets exhausting. Eventually you start resenting the relationship for the gap.

The Second Angle of the Mirror asks: who are you, really? Not in your best moments. Not in the version of yourself you want to be. Right now, as you are.

Honest answer to that question is the foundation of every good relationship I've seen last.
