# Image Prompts — The Horse Is the Traveling Star and It Carries Gold

**Topic:** Money and wealth in the Fire Horse year. Bold, fast-moving financial energy. Fire applied to earth produces metal (wealth). The Traveling Star carries gold for the prepared and punishes the cautious.

**Phrases:**
- "The Fire Horse doesn't reward caution. It rewards the ones who move."
- "Fire applied to earth produces metal."
- "The Traveling Star carries gold."
- "The window is open. The Horse doesn't wait."
- "Preparation plus boldness. Not one without the other."

## blog-fire-horse.md

**Concept:** A horseshoe struck in fire — the moment metal is forged from heat and impact. Raw power meeting precision. The elemental transformation that produces wealth.
**Image Style:** SCENE — dramatic close-up, forge aesthetic, dark and gold tones
**Aspect:** 16:9 (1200×630)

Prompt: A single horseshoe suspended in the air above glowing forge coals, orange and red embers scattered on a worn iron surface below. The horseshoe itself is dark iron, still warm from the forge, with a faint glow at its curves. Scattered around it on a dark stone surface: two or three antique gold coins, thick and irregular, the kind struck rather than printed. The light source is entirely the forge coals — deep amber and orange, casting hard shadows. The background is deep midnight charcoal, almost black. No text, no figures, no symbols, no watermarks. Palette: midnight charcoal, molten orange, antique gold, iron grey. Cinematic still. The feeling is power held in check — fire that has done its work and produced something lasting. No watermarks or Western zodiac imagery anywhere in the image.

## mon-instagram.md

**Concept:** Gold coins on fire — literal and visceral. Money meeting flame. The energy of the Fire Horse year condensed into a single powerful image.
**Image Style:** SCENE — flat lay, dramatic lighting
**Aspect:** 1:1 (1080×1080)

Prompt: Top-down overhead flat lay on a dark slate surface. A small cluster of thick antique gold coins — irregular, hand-struck, stacked slightly — sits at the center. From beneath one stack of coins, a single low flame licks upward, catching the edge of a coin in orange light. Around the coins: scattered embers, cooling but still faintly glowing. A single dried chrysanthemum — gold-orange, petals slightly open — rests to the upper left. The surface beneath is dark grey slate with fine natural grain. Palette: deep slate, molten gold, ember orange, dusty ivory for the flower. Natural overhead raking light. No text, no mahjong tiles, no zodiac imagery. Square format. No watermarks or Western zodiac imagery anywhere in the image.
