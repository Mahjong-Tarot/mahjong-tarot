Feel Good Friday: The wealth you want isn't waiting for the perfect moment. The Fire Horse rewards the ones who decide.

What financial move have you been putting off?

Not because you don't know enough. Not because the research isn't done. But because deciding feels permanent, and permanent feels scary.

This weekend, I'm asking you to do one thing. Write down the financial decision you've been holding — not your analysis, the actual decision. "I am going to..." or "I am not going to..."

Writing it makes it real. And in a Fire Horse year, real is where the energy goes.
