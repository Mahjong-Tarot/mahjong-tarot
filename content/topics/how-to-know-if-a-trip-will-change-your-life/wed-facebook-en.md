# Wednesday Facebook EN — How to Know If a Trip Will Change Your Life

Every trip is a decision. And every significant decision has a Central Theme — the one thing actually driving it, underneath everything else.

The Mahjong Mirror asks: what is your central theme for this trip?

Not the itinerary. Not the reason you tell people when they ask why you're going. The real one.

Are you moving toward something — a pull, an opening, a version of yourself that only exists somewhere new?

Or are you moving away from something — a relationship, a job, a version of your life that's stopped working?

Both are valid. The Fire Horse energy supports movement in either direction. But the outcome is completely different depending on which one is true, and whether you know it before you go.

Movement toward something in this energy is genuinely transformative. The people whose Central Theme is about growth or curiosity — those trips consistently deliver. The Horse meets them halfway.

Movement away from something is more complicated. The relief is real. The distance gives perspective. But if whatever you're running from is still at home when you get back — the trip won't fix it. Only clarity about the real Central Theme can do that.
