# Image Prompts — The Mahjong Mirror Way to Plan a Wedding

**Topic:** Wedding planning with intention — using the Mahjong Mirror framework to uncover the Central Theme driving a couple's choices and clarify what they truly want from the marriage, before a single vendor is booked.

**Phrases:**
- "Plan the marriage, not just the wedding."
- "Know what it's actually about."
- "The foundation holds. The party fades."
- "Tend the garden."
- "What do you really want?"
- "Build something neither of you could build alone."

## blog-mahjong-mirror.md

**Concept:** The blog's central image is a garden path — one half meticulously tended, the other half yielding to beautiful overgrowth. This captures the blog's core tension: the surface perfection of a curated wedding event versus the honest, persistent work of building a marriage. The image should feel quiet, warm, and real — not staged or decorative.

**Image Style:** SCENE — atmospheric nature scene used as emotional metaphor, no people

**Aspect:** 16:9 (1200x630)

Prompt: A narrow path winds through an old walled garden at late afternoon golden hour. One side of the path is immaculately tended — trimmed hedges, pale stone pavers, ivory and sage green plants in full bloom. The other side gradually yields to beautiful overgrowth — vines curling over stone, moss between the cracks, soft light filtering through tangled branches. The atmosphere is warm and contemplative, not ominous. Antique gold light rakes across the scene. No people visible. Color palette: antique gold, warm ivory, soft sage, deep green shadow. Film quality, slightly textured. No watermarks or Western zodiac imagery anywhere in the image.
