## Voice

The voice doc is the single source of truth for tone, cadence, and word-choice rules. It will live at [`./voice.md`](./voice.md) once written (Phase 3 W3.1).

Until that doc exists, voice principles are drafted across [`../product/product.md`](../product/product.md) §5 and §9.

Core principles, in shorthand:

- Probabilistic, not predictive
- Intelligent, not condescending
- No em-dashes; no exclamation points; no occult vocabulary unless required
- Sentence rhythm matters as much as content

## Images

Hero and gallery images for the live site live in `website/public/images/`. Source images (pre-WebP) live in `content/source-material/` until processed. The `working_files/` git-ignored directory is the staging area for images not yet referenced in a content draft.

A list of brand-approved imagery (vs. one-off content imagery) lives at [`./image-library.md`](./image-library.md) when populated.
