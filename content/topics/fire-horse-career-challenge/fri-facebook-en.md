# Friday Facebook EN — Fire Horse Career Challenge

**Weekend challenge:** If the Fire Horse year is an amplifier, what is it amplifying in your work life right now?

Satisfaction or frustration? Momentum or stagnation? Clarity about where you're going, or a nagging sense that something isn't adding up?

Write down the honest answer. Not the polished version. The one you'd say to yourself at 2am when no one else is listening.

That's your Central Theme. And it's the first step in the Mahjong Mirror framework.

Here's why this matters: most people skip the diagnostic. They jump straight to solutions — update the resume, have the conversation, make the move — without really sitting with what the situation is actually asking them to resolve.

The Fire Horse year is loud. Whatever the amplifier is pointing at, it's already making noise. The challenge this weekend is to stop and listen to it honestly instead of managing it or explaining it away.

One honest sentence. What is the Fire Horse amplifying in your career right now?

Write it down. And if you're willing to share it, post it below. You might be surprised how many people are sitting with the same thing.

The framework for what to do with that answer is in this week's blog posts and in The Mahjong Mirror. Links in comments.

Have a good weekend.
