## FAQ Schema

**Q: Does the Fire Horse year reward financial risk-takers?**
A: Yes — but specifically prepared, intentional risk-takers. The Fire Horse year (2026) amplifies both gains and losses. People who move with clarity and preparation tend to see strong results. Those who move recklessly face amplified losses.

**Q: How do I know if I'm ready to make a financial move in 2026?**
A: The key distinction is between readiness (have I done the preparation?) and certainty (do I feel confident?). Certainty is often unavailable in a volatile year. Readiness is a factual assessment of whether you've done the work.

**Q: What is the weekend financial challenge Bill Hajdu recommends?**
A: Write down the financial decision you've been holding — not your analysis, but the actual decision. Starting with "I am going to..." or "I am not going to..." forces the mind to treat it as real rather than theoretical.
