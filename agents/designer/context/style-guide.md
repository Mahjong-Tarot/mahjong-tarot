## Image Prompt Guidelines

When generating image prompts, push for maximum creative variety:

- **No repetitive motifs.** If tiles appear in one prompt, they don't need to appear in five more. One tile image is enough. Same for any single symbol — snakes, flames, candles, constellations.
- **Vary the medium.** Don't cluster around one style.
- **Vary the subject.** Not every image needs mahjong tiles or astrology symbols. Some should be purely emotional or conceptual — weather, shadows, objects, scenes that carry the idea without literal references.
- **When a concept has tension (danger vs. opportunity, risk vs. reward), explore multiple visual metaphors for that tension.** Don't settle on one split-screen treatment. Try: cracked vs. whole objects, storm vs. calm, tabloid vs. prestige, fire vs. water, destruction vs. construction.
- **Stretch beyond the obvious.** The first idea that comes to mind is the one every AI image tool would generate. Go past it. The best prompts make the viewer feel something before they understand the article's topic.
- **No text in images.** AI image generators cannot reliably render text. Never include words, titles, or typography in prompts. Text overlays are added separately in post-production.

## Style Inspiration

These are styles that work well for the brand. Use them as a starting point, not a checklist. Mix, combine, or invent new approaches as the concept demands.

### Photography / Human

- **The Oracle Portrait** — A woman from behind or in profile, never facing camera. Looking at tiles, standing in a doorway with light pouring through. Cinematic. She's the reader, not the subject.
- **Hands of the Reader** — Close-up of feminine hands interacting with tiles. Turning one over, holding one to candlelight. Warm skin tones against jade, silk, or dark wood.
- **The Ritual Scene** — Wider shot of a woman in a beautiful space, mid-ritual. Tea, candles, tiles laid out. Film still aesthetic.
- **The Morning Mirror** — A woman near a mirror in soft morning light, tiles reflected or scattered. Private, quiet, pre-day moment.
- **The Silhouette** — Woman in silhouette against a luminous backdrop. High contrast, dramatic.
- **The Gathering** — Two or three women around a table with tiles and tea. Caught mid-conversation. Warm, social, intimate.
- **The Walk Away** — Woman walking away from camera through a corridor or garden. Light pulls her forward.
- **Overhead Flat Lay** — Top-down shot of a reading in progress: hands, tiles, tea, journal, dried flowers.

### Atmospheric / Conceptual

- **The Parchment** — Aged warm parchment texture with faint gold filigree. Ancient document feel.
- **Neon Glow** — Thin elegant warm glow on a very dark background. Fortune teller's parlor at night. Subtle, not electric.
- **Split Composition** — Frame divided. One half solid color, other half atmospheric photo. Creates tension.
- **Nature as Metaphor** — Weather, water, fire, seasons, landscapes that carry the emotional weight of the topic without literal astrology references.
- **Object Still Life** — A single meaningful object (a tile, a ring, a letter, a cracked mirror) shot with cinematic lighting. The object tells the story.

## Non-Negotiables

- No generic stock photo aesthetic
- No cartoonish or clip-art mahjong tiles — tiles should feel like real objects with weight and history
- No culturally insensitive imagery (geisha stereotypes, "Oriental" tropes)
- No AI-robot imagery
- No watermarks or Western zodiac imagery
- Gold is warm (antique/brass), never chrome or silver
- Women in images never face the camera directly — mystery preserved
- Every image should feel like it belongs in the same world, even when the styles vary
