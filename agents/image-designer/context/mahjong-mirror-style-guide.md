# Mahjong Mirror — Image Style Guide

## Brand Context

Mahjong Mirror is a fortune-telling platform rooted in the symbolism of mahjong tiles. The audience skews female, spiritually curious, and drawn to beauty, mystery, and self-discovery. The visual language needs to feel **mystical but modern, feminine but not soft, ancient but alive.**

The brand sits at the intersection of:
- Eastern symbolism + Western digital delivery
- Spiritual depth + clean, premium aesthetics
- Personal ritual + scalable content

## Style 1: Mystical Glassmorphism

**Origin:** Futuristic Glassmorphism adapted for spiritual content.

**The look:** Translucent frosted glass layers with warm gold edge lighting. Mahjong tiles float behind glass panes. Soft gradient backgrounds shift from jade to plum. Symbols and tile faces are etched into glass surfaces.

**Use for:** Hero images, feature announcements, "your reading is ready" moments, landing page headers.

**Mood:** Elegant, luminous, otherworldly. Like looking through a temple window.

**Prompt direction:**
> Frosted translucent glass panels floating in soft darkness, warm gold rim lighting, mahjong tile symbols etched into glass surfaces, jade-to-plum mesh gradient background, volumetric fog, cinematic studio lighting, premium 3D render, no text, no people.

---

## Style 2: Botanical Oracle

**Origin:** Botanical Minimalism + spiritual symbolism.

**The look:** Clean layouts with real or illustrated botanical elements (bamboo, plum blossom, lotus, chrysanthemum) as frames and accents. Mahjong tiles sit among flowers and leaves. Heavy white/ivory space. Feels like a page from a beautiful journal.

**Use for:** Blog headers, email headers, social quotes, instructional content, seasonal reading promotions.

**Mood:** Serene, sophisticated, grounded. A quiet garden where wisdom lives.

**Prompt direction:**
> Minimal botanical composition on warm ivory background, delicate watercolor plum blossoms and bamboo leaves framing a single mahjong tile, soft natural light, generous negative space, muted sage and dusty rose accents, journal illustration aesthetic, no text.

---

## Style 3: Illustrated Line Art + Color Wash

**Origin:** Hand-drawn feminine illustration style.

**The look:** Confident single-weight line drawings of tiles, hands, tea ceremonies, moon phases, or symbolic scenes. Selective watercolor or gradient fills in gold, rose, sage, and plum. Feels handcrafted and personal.

**Use for:** Explainers ("how a reading works"), educational series, tile meaning breakdowns, social carousel content.

**Mood:** Approachable, artisanal, wise. A trusted guide drawing you a map.

**Prompt direction:**
> Single-weight ink line drawing of mahjong tiles arranged in a crescent moon pattern, selective watercolor washes in dusty rose and antique gold, warm ivory background, hand-drawn quality with slight imperfections, delicate and feminine, no text.

---

## Style 4: Bold Serif Editorial

**Origin:** Bold Serif Typography + Muted Palettes.

**The look:** Large elegant serif headlines (think Playfair Display, Cormorant, Lora energy) on cream, jade, or plum backgrounds. Minimal imagery. The words ARE the image. Feels like a high-end book cover or magazine feature.

**Use for:** Quote graphics, thought leadership posts, email subject teasers, announcement banners, Pinterest pins.

**Mood:** Authoritative, literary, magnetic. Fortune told in a whisper, not a shout.

**Prompt direction:**
> Elegant serif typography reading '[YOUR TEXT]' in antique gold on deep midnight jade background, subtle paper grain texture, thin gold decorative border, premium book cover aesthetic, no imagery, text-dominant composition.

---

## Style 5: Warm Editorial Photography

**Origin:** Natural light editorial adapted for spiritual/ritual content.

**The look:** Warm, intimate photographs of ritual moments: hands holding tiles, tea steam catching light, tiles arranged on silk or wood, candlelight on ceramic. Muted warm tones. Real textures. Shallow depth of field.

**Use for:** Instagram feed, blog hero images, "behind the mirror" brand storytelling, testimonial backgrounds.

**Mood:** Intimate, real, sacred. Like being invited into someone's private reading.

**Prompt direction:**
> Warm natural-light photograph of mahjong tiles arranged on dark linen cloth, shallow depth of field, soft golden afternoon light from a window, ceramic tea cup in background, muted warm color grading, cinematic and intimate, no people visible, no text.

---

## Style 6: Soft Gradient Abstract

**Origin:** Flowing organic gradients for modern feminine brands.

**The look:** Smooth flowing shapes with warm gradient transitions (ivory to rose, sage to jade, gold to plum). No hard edges. Tile silhouettes or symbols emerge subtly from the gradients. Feels like an aura or energy field.

**Use for:** App backgrounds, loading screens, meditation/reflection prompts, ambient social posts, story backgrounds.

**Mood:** Ethereal, calming, expansive. The feeling before a reading begins.

**Prompt direction:**
> Abstract flowing organic gradient composition, smooth transitions from dusty rose to sage green to soft gold, faint mahjong tile symbol silhouettes emerging from the gradient, no hard edges, luminous and ethereal, modern feminine aesthetic, no text.

---

## Style 7: Collage / Mixed Media Oracle

**Origin:** Editorial collage + mystical layering.

**The look:** Layered cutouts of vintage botanical prints, torn parchment edges, gold leaf fragments, mahjong tile photography, and celestial charts. Modern type or handwritten elements layered on top. Feels curated and mysterious.

**Use for:** Feature launches, seasonal campaigns, "collector" series content, premium email designs, Pinterest.

**Mood:** Eclectic, mysterious, premium. A fortune teller's desk turned into art.

**Prompt direction:**
> Mixed media collage composition, vintage botanical prints layered with torn parchment and gold leaf fragments, mahjong tile photograph centered, celestial star chart background, warm ivory and deep plum tones, editorial art aesthetic, rich textures, no text.

---

## Style 8: Napkin / Hand-Drawn Founder Sketch

**Origin:** Dave's napkin doodle style, adapted for behind-the-scenes content.

**The look:** Marker-on-paper sketches over real photos. Circled elements, arrows, handwritten notes. Feels like the founder scribbled their vision on a cafe napkin.

**Use for:** "Building in public" content, founder story posts, technical/architecture explainers, dev diary entries. NOT for customer-facing fortune content.

**Mood:** Raw, authentic, founder energy. The brain behind the mirror.

**Prompt direction:**
> Hand-drawn marker doodles layered over a warm photograph of a laptop and notebook on a wooden desk, confident imperfect pen strokes, navy and hot pink ink, arrows and circled elements with handwritten labels, napkin-sketch aesthetic, no people, warm natural light.

## Non-Negotiables

- No generic stock photo aesthetic
- No neon glow or cyberpunk tones
- No cartoonish or clip-art mahjong tiles
- No culturally insensitive imagery (geisha stereotypes, "Oriental" tropes)
- No em dashes in any visible text
- No AI-robot imagery
- No watermarks
- Tiles should feel like real objects with weight and history, not digital icons
- Gold is warm (antique/brass), never chrome or silver
- Every image should feel like it belongs in the same world
