# Unlock Your Destiny Through the *Mahjong Mirror*

A modern divination system inspired by ancient Mahjong symbolism — guiding you toward clarity, intuition, and deeper self-discovery.

## What Is The *Mahjong Mirror*?

The Mahjong Mirror is a divination method that transforms traditional Mahjong tiles into a symbolic language for self-reflection, where each tile becomes a portal representing energies, archetypes, situations, and life cycles, guiding you with clarity and intuition even if you've never played Mahjong before.

## The System: How Mahjong Becomes Divination

The Mahjong Mirror translates familiar tile imagery into a spiritual framework — a visual, intuitive form of divination rooted in ancient symbolism.

**The Three Suits**
Circles, Bamboos, and Characters reveal energy, growth, and identity.

**Honor Tiles**
Winds and Dragons act as powerful forces shaping your path.

**Tile Patterns**
Combinations function like tarot spreads, uncovering dynamics and life lessons.

**Intuitive Reading**
Teaches you to recognize patterns, themes, and synchronicities.
