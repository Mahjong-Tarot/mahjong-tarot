# Monday Facebook EN — The Horse Is the Traveling Star

Did you know that in Purple Star Astrology, the Chinese traveling star is literally called Ma — the Horse?

Not a coincidence that we're in the Year of the Fire Horse.

Ma is the heavenly stem that signals movement, journeys, displacement that changes things. When it shows up in a reading, something is moving. Usually a person. Usually in a direction they didn't fully plan.

In a normal year, Ma is a nudge. It favors people who act on it.

In the Fire Horse year — when the traveling star IS the ruling animal, doubled with fire energy — Ma isn't a nudge. It's a pull. The strongest travel energy in a 60-year cycle is running right now, and it will not come back in our lifetimes.

The people I'm watching move this year aren't the adventurers. They're accountants and teachers who suddenly bought tickets to places they'd been "meaning to go" for years. Retired couples calling me to say they're moving to Portugal. Entrepreneurs who've been talking about going international for a decade who just signed a lease.

That's what Ma on fire looks like.

The Horse doesn't care about distance. It cares about movement. A long weekend in a city you've never visited counts. Saying yes to the conference you'd normally skip counts. Getting in motion counts.
