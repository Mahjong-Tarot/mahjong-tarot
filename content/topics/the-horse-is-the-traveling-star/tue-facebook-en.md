# Tuesday Facebook EN — The Horse Is the Traveling Star

My wife Thanh didn't wait.

She knew the energy. She felt it. Vietnam, then Paris, then Montreal — not a grand tour, not a bucket list. Just movement that felt right, that opened things. That's what the Fire Horse year does when you stop fighting it.

I've been watching this pattern for 35 years. The people who thrive in Fire Horse years aren't the ones with the most detailed itinerary. They're the ones who said yes when the opportunity appeared. Booked the ticket before they were fully ready. Took the meeting in the city they'd never visited.

The Horse rewards movement, not planning.

Here's the thing nobody talks about: traveling in a Fire Horse year isn't just about the destination. The Ma energy — the traveling star — changes you in transit. Something shifts between where you left and where you arrive. People have described it to me as clarity they couldn't have gotten at home. Decisions that felt impossible for years that suddenly had obvious answers on the other side of a long flight.

The star that's running this year opens doors. But only for the people who move toward them.

Where are you going? Seriously. This week, this month, this year. What direction is the Horse pulling you?
