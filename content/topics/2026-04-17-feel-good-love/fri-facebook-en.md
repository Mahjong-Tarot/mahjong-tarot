Feel Good Friday: The best love story you'll ever live starts with knowing yourself. Not perfectly — just honestly.

This week we looked at Taylor and Travis — two Earth Fire Snakes who shouldn't work on paper but do in practice. We looked at the Mahjong Mirror and how the First Angle gives a wedding its meaning. But the thread that ties it all together? The Second Angle. Know Thyself.

Before you can be the right partner, you have to see yourself clearly. Not the version you perform. Not the version you wish you were. The one who's actually sitting at the table.

That's what the Mirror is for. It doesn't judge. It reflects. And what it shows you — your strengths, your blind spots, the patterns you keep repeating — that's the foundation everything else gets built on. Love included.

Here's your weekend challenge: Look in the mirror. Not the one on the wall — the one inside. Ask yourself one honest question about who you are in your relationships. Write it down. Sit with the answer.

That's where real love starts. Not with finding someone. With knowing yourself well enough to show up as the person worth finding.
