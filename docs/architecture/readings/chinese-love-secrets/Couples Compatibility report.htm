  Introduction
So you want to know if you have found the perfect match?! Or, maybe things haven’t turned out the way you thought, and you are now wondering if your partner is really a native of planet Earth. Well, you better have a seat, and maybe a stiff drink. You are about to read a no holes-barred analysis. Is it Cupid or Loki (God of Mischief) who has loosed that arrow? The answer may surprise you.
One thing you probably do know is relationships are complicated. Not to worry. Chinese Astrology is more than capable of revealing what you need to know. You no doubt will be happy to look at that one number that gives you an overall rating of your match. Yes, there is one, and you will see it soon. If it's a scorching 98% favorable, you can put aside the report for rainy day reading and go celebrate. More likely than not, however, it's going to raise more questions than it answers, either by being a lot closer to a so-so, 50% favorable rating, or even less.
Unfortunately this is the more usual situation. Drat. If your relationship is at all normal, it has its good and bad points. You will be able to pick them out as you read the following pages. There might even be times when a comment contradicts something you just read. Are you surprised? Some days things could be just grand between you and your partner. Then there are others when you question how sane you were when you said yes. Or, maybe someone else has taken over your partner's body. Life is complicated, and you just have to deal with it.
But, help is at hand. This report will provide you with the same kinds of information Chinese grandmothers have used to help arrange marriages for more than a thousand years. Say hi to grandma as she now begins to dispense her wisdom.
Drum roll please. Chinese Astrology says Lori and Brad have a compatibility rating of 78 % favorable. And that means …?
Don’t panic-no cause for alarm. It is just that there are two things you need to know.
The first is the number, 78%, is an average. You can’t ignore the other 22%. Some of what it represents is going to cause you grief. On the other hand, there will be times when you feel on top of the world, perhaps experiencing a 98% on the day.
Generally this match is likely to contribute to your happiness the majority of the time. The same goes for your partner. Usually you will be glad you came together. Once in a while that won’t be the case. It’s up to both of you to keep in mind the good things about each other. If you do that, the relationship should last and bring you the happiness you seek, as the good will outweigh the bad.
The second is you are two, different people. No, not talking about one of you being from Mars and the other Venus. Face it; you’re normal. Generally speaking any two people are not going to be clones, even those who have fallen in love and come to believe they can’t live without each other.
With moderately good compatibility you will be able to satisfy most of each other’s needs---but not all. You probably understand each other most of the time---but not always. You generally complement each other’s strengths and weakness---but not completely. You two communicate well---usually. True, this can occasionally cause problems. On the other hand it can also be a source of pleasure. Everyone likes something different at times. It could be boring if all people were alike. One other point. Your differences can also add depth to the relationship, for you each bring a unique viewpoint to the partnership.
You both just have to remember the numbers are in your favor! One thing you can do is to carefully read the sign descriptions in the Appendix. It will provide many keys to understanding your partner as well as yourself.
So you want details. How about taking a look at your signs in combination.
Keep in mind Chinese Astrology signs have two parts, an element and an animal component.
This is significant as each has something important to say about your relationship. Animal combinations reveal how well you are likely to get along with each other, can satisfy each other’s needs, and achieve mutual understanding. Will your love deepen, or will you get on each other’s nerves as time passes? The elements disclose how good you are for each other. In other words, will you nourish, be lucky for, and bring out the best in your partner and vice versa?
Voila! Here’s your answer at a glance.

Tiger (Lori ’s Year Sign)

To understand how the Tiger operates, it is important to know Tiger is a Yang and a heart sign.

Being a Yang sign person, you are most effective using a direct, if not aggressive, approach to life. You tend to be proactive.

As a heart sign person, you are most comfortable following your heart rather than basing decisions on logic. This fits perfectly with your passionate nature.

Positive Traits:

Adventurous, affectionate, charitable, colorful, courageous, dynamic, empathetic, exciting, humorous, optimistic, playful and resolute.The adventurous and courageous Tiger is a born leader. You are not afraid to blaze new trails, and, given your independent streak, you are more than willing to go it alone if necessary. Your firm determination also makes you a fierce competitor.

Your passion also gives you the potential to be a star performer. Colorful and dynamic, you have a commanding presence before any group. Yes, we can count on you to command our attention and generate excitement.

Interestingly, you have another side, that of the playful kitten. It is characterized not only by youthful innocence and optimism but also a playful sense of humor. You enjoy telling jokes, which are all the more funny because people usually see your serious side and are caught off guard to see you out of character.

Your kinder and gentler side also includes a genuine humanitarian streak. You are empathetic and keenly sense the emotions and feelings of others, to which you respond with compassion and a sincere desire to help when needed.

Negative Traits:

Anxious, argumentative, authoritarian, critical, domineering, edgy, impatient, impulsive, narrow minded, rash, ruthless, self centered and temperamental.

The downside of the Tiger's passion is that it can lead to emotional distress, especially when frustrated by people or events. You have a tendency to be anxious and edgy. This uneasiness of mind could come from brooding fear about some contingency or simply a feeling of being tense or on edge.

Your high energy level fueled by passion often makes you impatient. You want to do things right away and can become deeply frustrated with unplanned delays. Your rashness sometimes manifests itself as impulsive behavior. There is a bit of the fools rush in mentality in you. In other words, you do not always think things through before you act.

You have a tendency to be self centered; so, this hasty action occasionally fails to take into consideration its affect on others. Your focus on yourself can manifest itself in a form of narrow mindedness as contempt for the interests of others. You also can hurt others by being overly critical and argumentative when they do not perform up to the high standards you set for yourself. And, you almost demand that they do. Your sign is a strong one, and it is not surprising you have a tendency to be authoritarian and domineering. At your worst, you can even be ruthless. It is not wise to cross you deliberately.

Do not be surprised if the Snake takes what appears to be a personal interest in you. They are happy to learn about your experiences and to have you share their knowledge with them. In turn, you will be totally captivated by their magnificence and hang on their every word. Beware! The Snake's appetite is insatiable. They will tire of you and turn to other interests long before you are ready to end the relationship.

The Snake is basically a private person; so, it is not likely you are going to be continually running into them at social functions. In fact, more often than not they enjoy their own company more than that of other people. They easily lose themselves in their thoughts and derive great pleasure from so doing.

All in all, you are relatively harmless when it comes to others, but it is a different story when it comes to yourself. When apparently overcome by events you can enter into your self destructive mode. It can manifest itself as gluttony or just plain sadness. You are easily disheartened when slandered and saddened when your feelings are hurt, you experience disappointments, or just see others being sad. At your worst you engage in wanton pursuit of pleasure.
