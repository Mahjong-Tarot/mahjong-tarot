# Phase 6 — Book & 1-on-1 Practice

**Window:** Q2–Q3 2027
**Status:** ☐ Planned
**Validation gate:** Phases 1–5 closed; ≥ 500 active paid members

---

## Goal

The book is integrated as a deepening mechanism (not the front door). Bill's 1-on-1 practice runs inside the product as the high-margin roof layer. Share loops mature into a measurable acquisition channel.

By Q3 2027 close: ≥ 30% of book buyers start a member trial; ≥ 20% of trials convert to paid; Bill's session prep time drops from ~30 min to ≤ 10 min.

---

## Why this phase exists

Per [`product.md`](./product.md) §5, the book ships as a *deepening* mechanism. Per the same section, the 1-on-1 reading practice is the **roof**, not the floor. By the time someone books an hour with Bill, they have spent 60 days in the system, they have vocabulary, and the conversation can start at minute one instead of minute thirty.

---

## Workstreams

### W6.1 — Digital book reader

- Web-based, member-gated reader for the digital edition
- Cross-references from book chapters to live readings ("see your own Purple Star chart")
- Print edition fulfilment integrated (Lulu / Bookvault TBD)

**Owner:** web-developer agent
**Definition of done:** Member opens chapter 4 → tapping a reference launches their own Purple Star reading inline.

---

### W6.2 — Book → trial flow for non-members

- Sample-chapter landing page for paid acquisition
- Purchase → member trial bundled (digital edition free with trial; print edition shipped + trial)
- Conversion tracking for the trial → paid step

**Owner:** Dave Hajdu + web-developer agent
**Definition of done:** ≥ 30% of book buyers start a member trial within 14 days; ≥ 20% of trials convert to paid.

---

### W6.3 — In-product 1-on-1 booking

- Calendar surface (Bill's availability via Cal.com or similar)
- Payment for session (bundled or à la carte)
- Pre-session brief auto-generated from member's chart and recent readings
- Cohort summary for Bill ("here's what's coming up this week")

**Owner:** web-developer agent + Bill
**Definition of done:** Bill's pre-session prep time drops from ~30 min to ≤ 10 min; session NPS ≥ 70.

---

### W6.4 — Share & acquisition maturation

- OG image generator polished for "Find a Good Day" + Compatibility
- Referral mechanic (founder gets 1 month free per invited paying member)
- Light paid acquisition lever to top up share-led growth

**Owner:** Dave Hajdu + web-developer agent
**Definition of done:** ≥ 30% of paid signups originate from share loops by Q4 2026 (yes — this gate measures earlier than Phase 6 closes; it tracks momentum from Phase 2 onward).

---

## What's explicitly out of scope this phase

- Native iOS / Android app (PWA only)
- Mobile-only feature parity break (everything works on web)
- Public/social profiles (never)
- Open-ended AI chatbot (never)
- Corporate / employer wellness sales (different audience, different motion)

---

## Risks

- Book launch coordination — print fulfilment, digital DRM (or lack thereof — open question), and trial wiring all hit a single window. Plan with at least 6 weeks of buffer.
- Bill's 1-on-1 practice runs outside the product today; migrating it inside without breaking his workflow needs a careful rollout.
- Referral mechanic can leak revenue if poorly attributed. Audit every payout.

---

## Exit criteria → "operational" mode

After Phase 6, the project moves into operational mode: monthly metric review, opportunistic feature work driven by founder feedback, and an annual strategy revisit. There is no Phase 7. The 12-month shape from `product.md` §5 is fully built.

---

## What gets revisited in 12 months

- The thesis itself ([`product.md`](./product.md))
- Audience definition and fit (still her, or has she changed?)
- The two metrics (D7 return, M6 paid retention) — still the right ones?
- Whether Bill is still the bottleneck for any layer of the product
