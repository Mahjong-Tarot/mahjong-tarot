# Interview Notes: Career in the Year of the Fire Horse
**Source:** Bill interview, April 19, 2026
**Topic:** Chinese Astrology — Career outlook for the Fire Horse year (2026)

---

## The Overall Career Energy

The Fire Horse is fast, dynamic, and unforgiving of those who are passive. This is not a year to sit back and wait. Action is favored. The year's energy rewards those who step up, stand out, and channel their effort with focus and intention.

The job market itself is not great — but the Fire Horse year has tremendous speed and energy. Opportunities will appear and disappear quickly. Being alert, prepared, and focused is everything.

---

## For Job Seekers

The key three traits:
1. **Alert** — opportunities move fast; you have to be watching for them
2. **Prepared** — when the opportunity comes, you must be ready to act immediately
3. **Focused** — scattered energy leads to missed chances and wasted effort

Half efforts won't cut it. 110% effort is what creates results this year.

---

## For People Already Employed

The Fire Horse year is not the time to respond — it's the time to **lead.** Those who are reactive will get left behind. Those who are proactive will get noticed, progress, and thrive.

**The core challenge:** You have to be both aggressive and cautious at the same time. Channel the fire energy into the right places — don't burn yourself out, but don't sit still either.

**The role of AI:** This is a year where leveraging tools like AI to multiply your productivity is a real competitive advantage. Those who can harness AI to increase output will stand out.

---

## 3 Things To Do This Year (Career)

1. **Volunteer for a visible project** — get in front of something that the boss and coworkers can see. Even if others execute it, be the driving force. Lead it or be the inspiration behind it.

2. **Use fire's creative spark to create something new** — come up with an idea or solution that adds value to yourself and your organization. Example: learn to build an AI agent to help with your job. If it's successful, you could become the example for the whole organization.

3. **Do something that makes your boss look good** — figure out what that looks like in your specific situation, and do it. This one works any year, but especially in a Fire Horse year.

---

## 3 Things To Avoid This Year (Career)

1. **Overextending yourself** — the pace is fast and mistakes multiply when you take on more than you can handle. Don't promise more than you can deliver.

2. **Starting new things you're not prepared for** — be careful about "the new." You want to be doing things, but starting unprepared new initiatives is a setup for failure.

3. **Pushing for change you don't have to make** — don't go looking for a new job if you don't need to. Don't suggest other people change their ways. Don't overhaul how things are done unless it's truly necessary.

---

## Who Thrives in Their Career This Year

**Characteristics:**
- Proactive, not reactive
- Decisive — can act quickly without needing excessive deliberation
- Able to maintain focus even amid fast-moving chaos
- Willing to step outside their comfort zone
- Good self-control — doesn't burn out or get swept up in every opportunity

**Signs that tend to do well:**
- **Tiger** — bold, aggressive, prepared
- **Dog** — calculated, strategic, responsible, focused

---

## Who Struggles in Their Career This Year

**Characteristics:**
- Needs a lot of time to think before acting
- Overly cautious or indecisive
- Easily loses confidence when things move fast
- Prone to starting too many things and finishing none
- Cannot maintain focus

**Signs that may struggle:**
- **Monkey** — always thinking; can lose confidence easily when opportunities slip by. However, if in tech and can maintain focus, can still do well.
- **Rat** — tends to get stuck in their head; prone to taking on too much risk
- **Rabbit** — fast pace is not their natural element
- **Metal-year people** — should avoid major career changes; wait for the fire energy to pass
