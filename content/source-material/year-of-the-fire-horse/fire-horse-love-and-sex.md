# Love & Sex in the Year of the Fire Horse

## The Core Message

This is a double fire year, so the energy supporting passion is exceptionally strong. Use it. If you're in a relationship, raise it to the next level — make a confession, propose, get married, have a baby. If you're struggling, throw passion at the problem and see if you can turn things around. If you're single, get in the game. The horse is Yang — be proactive, don't wait.

## Your Partner Wants Passion Too

The partner dynamic is critical this year. Your partner is going to be feeling this passionate energy just as much as you are. If your partner doesn't feel like they're getting as much passion, as much attention as they want, this is the year that you could lose your partner. Nurture the relationship actively. Tend the garden.

## Sex

Passion in the bedroom is really great. This year is a great year for having sex. With your partner, you definitely want to make sure that your partner is being satisfied and your partner is satisfying you. Enjoy the energy.

## Finding a Lasting Relationship

For people looking for love, when you hit it this year, you hit it big. People always want to know about lasting relationships, and in the year of the Fire Horse, there's a very good chance you're going to find one — and when it happens, it happens in a big way.

## Love by Sign

**Rat** — Unfavorable year. High risk. Must actively tend the relationship and pay close attention to keep things going well.

**Ox** — Same as the rat. The ox energy does not go well with the horse energy. Must really pay attention to their love life.

**Tiger** — Very favorable. Go all out to raise your level of involvement and the depth of your love. Expect proposals, marriages, and babies.

**Rabbit / Cat** — Neutral, but will struggle energetically to keep up. The key advice is simply to keep up with your partner.

**Dragon** — Neither good nor bad. Tends to get caught up in big schemes and forget about the partner. Pay attention to your partner.

**Snake** — Mixed fortune. The snake tends to go deep inside rather than outward. Must be more outgoing than usual and get out of its comfort zone.

**Horse** — A challenging year because of too much of a good thing. The horse's own freedom-loving energy gets amplified, risking neglect of the partner. Must really focus on the partner's needs and making sure they get enough passion.

**Sheep / Goat** — Exceptionally happy year. The goat is the soulmate sign of the horse. Expect many proposals, marriages, and babies.

**Monkey** — Mixed. Good and bad opportunities. Drop the mind games. This is a time for passion, not strategy.

**Rooster** — Gets along fairly decently with the horse. But the rooster's tendency to be picky needs to be released. Let your emotions and passions go and really put something into your love life.

**Pig** — Challenging year. Not unlucky, but requires real attention to relationships. Lean into your naturally sensual nature and put passion into it — you can do well.
