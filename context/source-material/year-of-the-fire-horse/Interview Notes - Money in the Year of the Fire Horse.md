# Interview Notes: Money in the Year of the Fire Horse
**Source:** Bill interview, April 19, 2026
**Topic:** Chinese Astrology — Financial outlook for the Fire Horse year (2026)

---

## The Overall Energy of the Fire Horse Year

The Fire Horse is fast-paced, high-energy, and unforgiving. Think Genghis Khan — take no prisoners, things happen fast and all the way. It is a high-risk, high-action year.

From a Chinese astrology perspective, the Fire Horse is generally a **good year for business, career, investment, and making money.** Fire has tremendous dynamism and energy to get things done. Fire also has a constructive relationship with earth — fire applied to earth produces metal, which is synonymous with money and wealth.

The year favors people who are **prepared.** The Year of the Snake (2025) was the time for making plans. Those who used that time well are positioned to win big in 2026.

---

## Who Will Benefit Financially

**Characteristics that thrive:**
- Bold, prepared, decisive action-takers
- People who can operate in a fast-paced environment
- Entrepreneurs and risk-takers (with preparation)
- Those born in **Earth years** — fire is constructive to earth, producing metal (money/wealth). Earth-year people may be able to take slightly more risk than usual and can expect stronger gains, especially if they are down-to-earth, practical, and have their plans together.

**Signs that tend to benefit:**
- **Tiger** — bold, aggressive, not afraid to take risks
- **Dog** — bold in a more calculated, strategic way; pragmatic, focused, responsible
- **Sheep/Goat** — the soulmate sign of the Horse; predicted to have one of the best financial years of all signs (though also one of the highest chances of failure — most sheep will do well, but those who stumble could fall hard)

---

## Who Will Struggle Financially

**Characteristics that struggle:**
- The timid and unprepared — opportunities will pass them by
- Overthinkers who can't act quickly and decisively
- People who jump in without preparation and take reckless risks
- Those who can't maintain focus — the Horse has a short attention span and this energy is contagious

**Signs that tend to struggle:**
- **Metal-year people** — fire destroys metal, which means wealth can melt away. Avoid major new projects and changes; if you must start something, do it slowly and deliberately. Do not take financial risks you don't have to. May need to wait two years (two fire years in a row) for big moves.
- **Rat** — water sign, supposedly its worst year for money in a Horse year. Water rats especially should avoid risk. Prone to overthinking and getting stuck in their head.
- **Rabbit/Cat** — fast pace is not their thing, though a lucky money star in their chart softens the impact
- **Pig** — tends to go with the flow rather than being prepared; coming off an already difficult Snake year for money

---

## Key Financial Risks This Year

1. **Taking too much risk** — the #1 challenge. The energy of the year will push people toward risk-taking, even those who shouldn't.
2. **Burnout from overextension** — the year makes you feel like your energy is unlimited. It isn't. Biting off more than you can chew will cost you.
3. **Starting unprepared new projects** — especially dangerous for metal-year people and anyone who hasn't done their planning homework.
